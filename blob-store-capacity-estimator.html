About This Tool

This tool helps estimate the number of Azure Storage Accounts needed based on your daily ingress volume. Each storage account has a maximum ingress throughput of 60 GB/sec.

The calculator determines how many storage accounts you need to stay below the ingress limit and provides a 6-month growth projection assuming 10% monthly growth.
